Checkers Rules and Gameplay

Checkers is a fun, challenging, game!!!!!

Checkers is a board game played between two people on an 8x8 checked board.

Each player has 12 pieces. The pieces are placed on every other dark square and then staggered by rows as shown on the board.

Each Checkers player has different coloured pieces. Sometimes the pieces are black and red or red and white.

Turns

The darker colour pieces move first. Each player takes their turn by moving a piece. Pieces are always moved diagonally and can be moved in the following ways:

  • Diagonally in the forward direction (towards the opponent) to the next dark square.
